# Licensed User MFA Report

This report filters the MFA view to only show users who have Microsoft 365 licenses assigned — meaning they actively use email, Teams, OneDrive, or other company services. Unlicensed accounts (service accounts, disabled users) are excluded.

Licensed users represent the accounts most likely to interact with sensitive company data on a daily basis. Ensuring they all have MFA is one of the most cost-effective security investments you can make.
