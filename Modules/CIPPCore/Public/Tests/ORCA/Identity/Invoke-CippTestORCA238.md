Safe Links should be enabled for Office applications to protect users from malicious URLs in Office documents such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint files. This protection extends URL scanning to documents opened in Office desktop, mobile, and web applications, helping prevent users from clicking malicious links embedded in documents.

**Remediation action**

- [Set up Safe Links policies in Microsoft Defender for Office 365](https://learn.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/security/office-365-security/safe-links-policies-configure)
- [Safe Links in Microsoft Defender for Office 365](https://learn.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/security/office-365-security/safe-links-about)
