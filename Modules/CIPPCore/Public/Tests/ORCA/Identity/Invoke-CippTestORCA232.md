Each accepted domain in your organization should be covered by a malware filter policy to ensure all users receive protection from malicious attachments and files. Without domain-specific malware filtering, users in certain domains may be exposed to viruses, trojans, and other malware delivered through email, creating potential entry points for security breaches.
