Anti-spam policies should enable Zero Hour Autopurge (ZAP) for spam messages to automatically detect and move spam messages that were delivered before they were identified as spam. ZAP for spam helps keep user mailboxes clean by moving spam messages to the Junk Email folder after delivery, even if they initially passed spam filtering.

**Remediation action**
