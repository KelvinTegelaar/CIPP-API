Anti-spam policies should be configured to move spam messages to the Junk Email folder or quarantine them to protect users from potentially malicious content. Setting the spam action to "Move to Junk Email Folder" or "Quarantine" ensures that spam is properly filtered while still allowing users to review quarantined messages if needed.
