Enhanced filtering should be configured on inbound connectors when mail flows through third-party services before reaching Exchange Online. This feature preserves important email authentication signals and source IP information that would otherwise be lost, enabling proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC validation as well as accurate spam and phishing detection.
