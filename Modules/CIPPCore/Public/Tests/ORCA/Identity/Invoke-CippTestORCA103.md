Outbound spam filter policies should be configured with appropriate limits to prevent compromised accounts from sending large volumes of spam. Microsoft recommends setting RecipientLimitExternalPerHour to 500, RecipientLimitInternalPerHour to 1000, and ActionWhenThresholdReached to BlockUserForToday to protect your organization's reputation and prevent abuse.
