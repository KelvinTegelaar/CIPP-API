Safe Attachments policies should configure the unknown malware response to block messages containing potentially malicious attachments. This setting ensures that messages with attachments that cannot be verified as safe are blocked from delivery, preventing zero-day malware attacks and protecting users from unknown threats until they can be properly analyzed.
