Anti-phishing policies should configure mailbox intelligence-based impersonation protection to move messages to the Junk Email folder. This setting ensures that messages identified as potential impersonation attempts are quarantined or moved to junk mail, preventing users from being exposed to sophisticated social engineering attacks while still allowing for message review if needed.
