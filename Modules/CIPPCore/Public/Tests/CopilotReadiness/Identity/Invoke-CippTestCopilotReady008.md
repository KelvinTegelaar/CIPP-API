# Copilot Candidate Tier Breakdown

Not all licensed users will benefit equally from Microsoft 365 Copilot — users who are already active across Teams, Outlook, and Office apps will experience immediate productivity gains, while users with low M365 engagement may find Copilot unhelpful until they build core habits first.

This informational test scores each licensed user across six readiness signals from the Microsoft 365 Copilot Readiness report (30-day window): Copilot license assigned, qualified update channel, Teams meetings, Teams chat, Outlook email, and Office document usage. Users scoring 4 or more signals are **High** tier candidates with strong Copilot ROI potential, 3 signals are **Medium** tier, and 2 or fewer are **Low** tier where adoption work should come before Copilot deployment.

Use this breakdown to prioritize Copilot license assignments, target adoption campaigns, and set realistic expectations for rollout phases.
