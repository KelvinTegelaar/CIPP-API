# Users Are Actively Using Microsoft Teams

Microsoft 365 Copilot in Teams provides intelligent meeting recaps, action item extraction, real-time meeting summaries, and chat thread summaries. These are among the highest-value Copilot scenarios — but they only apply to users who actively use Teams for meetings and chat.

This test uses the Microsoft 365 Copilot Readiness report (30-day window) to check the percentage of licensed users who attended a Teams meeting or sent a Teams chat message in the past 30 days. A threshold of 50% is used — if fewer than half of licensed users are active in Teams, meeting and chat Copilot features will have limited reach.

**Remediation action**
- [Copilot in Microsoft Teams overview](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-in-teams)
- [Drive Teams adoption in your organization](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-teams/)
- [Teams meetings adoption resources](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/productivty-library/meetings-calling/)
