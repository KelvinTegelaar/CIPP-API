Litigation hold and retention policies prevent permanent loss of mailbox content from accidental or malicious deletion. While not a true backup, they provide point-in-time recovery for E8 ML1.

**Remediation Action**

1. Apply a Microsoft 365 retention policy to user mailboxes covering Exchange, OneDrive, and SharePoint.
2. Or enable per-mailbox litigation hold with a duration of at least the organisation's retention requirement.
