A device that has not synced with Intune for two weeks is invisible to MDM-based patching and detection. Patch state cannot be enforced or measured for these endpoints.

**Remediation Action**

1. Contact users of the listed devices and ensure they bring them online and sign in.
2. If a device has been offline >30 days, retire/wipe it from Intune and re-enrol when returned.
