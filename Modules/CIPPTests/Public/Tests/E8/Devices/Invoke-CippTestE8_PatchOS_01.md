Windows Update for Business is the cloud-native way to deliver OS quality and feature updates. At least one Update Ring policy must be deployed and assigned for E8 patch-OS controls to be measurable.

**Remediation Action**

1. Intune > Devices > Windows > Update rings for Windows 10 and later > Create.
2. Set service channel, deferral periods, and deadlines (see ML2 quality deferral test).
3. Assign to all Windows devices.
