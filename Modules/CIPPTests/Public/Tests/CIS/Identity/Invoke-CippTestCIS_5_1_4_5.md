Without LAPS, the same local administrator password is often used on every device, turning a single compromised endpoint into lateral movement across the fleet.

**Remediation Action**

Microsoft Entra admin centre > Devices > Device settings > Enable Microsoft Entra Local Administrator Password Solution: **Yes**. Then deploy an Intune Account Protection LAPS policy.
