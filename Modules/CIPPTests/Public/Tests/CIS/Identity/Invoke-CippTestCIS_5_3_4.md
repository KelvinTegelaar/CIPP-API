Approval-gated GA activation prevents a single compromised admin from activating the role unilaterally. A second approver is required, raising the bar for an attacker.

**Remediation Action**

Microsoft Entra > PIM > Microsoft Entra roles > Global Administrator > Settings: Require approval to activate = Yes, approvers = (named GA list).
