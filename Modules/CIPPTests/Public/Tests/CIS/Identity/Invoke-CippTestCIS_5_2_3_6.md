System-preferred MFA always offers the user their strongest registered method, defeating attacks that try to downgrade them to a weaker option (SMS, voice).

**Remediation Action**

Microsoft Entra > Authentication methods > Settings: System-preferred multifactor authentication = Enabled, Include = All users.
