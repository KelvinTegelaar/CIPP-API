Microsoft Purview DLP detects and prevents accidental disclosure of sensitive data (PII, financial, health) across Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive and Teams.

**Remediation Action**

Use the Purview compliance portal to create at least one DLP policy targeting your sensitive information types and switch the policy to *Turn on*.
