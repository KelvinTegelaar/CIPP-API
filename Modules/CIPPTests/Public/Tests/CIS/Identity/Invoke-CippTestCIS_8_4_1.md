Teams apps run with broad permissions. CIS recommends an explicit allow-list of third-party apps managed via permission policies.

**Remediation Action**

Teams admin centre > Teams apps > Permission policies > Global — set Third-party apps to Block all apps (or Allow specific apps and add the approved list).
