A compromised user can register a rogue device under their identity, satisfy compliance, and use the device as a foothold. Restrict joins to a small set of helpdesk / provisioning accounts.

**Remediation Action**

Microsoft Entra admin centre > Devices > Device settings > Users may join devices to Microsoft Entra: Selected (and add the helpdesk group) or None.
