# Tenant License Overview

This report gives you a clear picture of the Microsoft 365 licenses in your tenant — what you own, what's being used, and where there may be room to optimize. Think of it as a quick health check on your software subscriptions.

Licenses at high utilization may need attention before new hires or projects push you over the limit. Unused licenses are an opportunity to reduce costs.
