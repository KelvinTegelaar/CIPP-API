# Tenant Capabilities Report

This report lists every service plan and feature available in your Microsoft 365 tenant based on your active subscriptions. Think of it as an inventory of what your licenses actually include.

Many organizations are unaware of the full range of tools they already have access to. This report can help you discover features you're paying for but not yet using — such as advanced security tools, compliance controls, or analytics capabilities.

Capabilities are grouped by category to make it easier to understand what's available at a glance.
