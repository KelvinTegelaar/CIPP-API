# Copilot Active User Count by App

Knowing which Microsoft 365 apps are driving Copilot engagement — and which are underutilized — helps focus adoption efforts. A tenant where users are active in Copilot for Teams but not Word or Excel may benefit from targeted training on document-based Copilot features, and vice versa.

This informational test retrieves aggregate active user counts per Copilot app for the past 30 days. No pass/fail is applied — the data is presented to give a high-level view of where Copilot is gaining traction across the tenant.

**Remediation action**
- [Copilot in Microsoft Teams](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-in-teams)
- [Copilot in Outlook](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-in-outlook)
- [Copilot in Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and OneNote](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/microsoft-365/copilot-in-word)
