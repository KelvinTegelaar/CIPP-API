# Copilot Adoption Trend (7-Day)

A declining trend in active Copilot users can be an early warning sign of user disengagement — users may have tried Copilot initially but failed to integrate it into their daily workflows. Catching this early allows administrators and MSPs to intervene with targeted training, adoption campaigns, or champion programs before disengagement becomes entrenched.

This informational test compares the active Copilot user count at the start and end of the past 7-day window. An upward trend indicates healthy, growing adoption. A flat trend is acceptable. A downward trend is flagged as noteworthy and may warrant investigation or an adoption intervention.

**Remediation action**
- [Copilot Success Kit and adoption resources](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/)
- [Identify and enable Copilot champions](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/enabling-champions/)
- [Microsoft Viva Insights for Copilot adoption](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/viva/insights/advanced/analyst/templates/microsoft-365-copilot-adoption)
- [Microsoft 365 Copilot usage report](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/admin/activity-reports/microsoft-365-copilot-usage)
