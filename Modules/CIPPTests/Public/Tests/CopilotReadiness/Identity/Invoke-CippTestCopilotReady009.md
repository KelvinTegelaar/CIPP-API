# Majority of Users Are Copilot-Ready (Medium or Above)

A Copilot rollout delivers the best return on investment when most users are already engaged with the Microsoft 365 apps that Copilot enhances. When a large share of users have low M365 engagement, Copilot licenses may go underutilized, and the deployment may generate negative sentiment if users cannot see the value.

This test uses the same 6-signal scoring as the candidate tier breakdown (test CopilotReady008) and checks whether at least 70% of licensed users score Medium (3 signals) or above. Tenants that fail this test should consider running an M365 adoption campaign focused on Teams, Outlook, and OneDrive/SharePoint before or alongside a Copilot rollout.

**Remediation action**
- [Microsoft 365 adoption hub](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/)
- [Copilot Success Kit — change management resources](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/copilot/)
