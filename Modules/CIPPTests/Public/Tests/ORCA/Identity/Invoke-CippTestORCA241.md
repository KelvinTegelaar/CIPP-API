First Contact Safety Tips display warnings when users receive email from a sender for the first time. This feature helps users recognize potentially suspicious messages from unfamiliar senders, providing an opportunity to verify the sender's identity before responding or clicking links, especially useful for detecting spear-phishing attacks from new contacts.
