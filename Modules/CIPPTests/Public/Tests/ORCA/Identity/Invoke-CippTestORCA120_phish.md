Anti-spam policies should enable Zero Hour Autopurge (ZAP) for phishing messages to automatically detect and remediate phishing attacks that were delivered before they were identified as malicious. ZAP for phishing helps protect users from newly identified phishing campaigns by moving or deleting malicious messages from mailboxes after delivery.

**Remediation action**
